3 steps to enjoy this recipe book

1. Looking for sweets that catch your attention.
Tapping a tab named 'Recipes' leads you to an index page (The tab is on the top banner. If it is a cell phone, just tap a cupcake on the banner and it will show up. Or you can find it at the very bottom as well.). Then, please go over all the recipes and choose whatever you like.

2. Reading the recipes.
Jumping to a recipe page, you can read through the whole directions and learn how it is made. Each page has got a link to a recipe creator's SNS or homepage, so you can visit there, too. By the way, flipping a page number at the bottom right of each page leads you to the previous or next recipe directly.

3. Well, that's it.
If you feel like actually cooking the sweets, the third step will be like purchasing ingredients or starting from the first direction right away. That must be fun. But if you do not have time or energy (or maybe just too lazy like me), that's totally okay! Please just read the recipes like reading a book and have fun!

Let's dive into the world of sweets!
