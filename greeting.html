Welcome to the Greeting Page!

Hi, I am Sawa. Thank you for visiting this Greeting Page.
This time I managed to pre-complete this recipe book, being helped by many people. I appreciate their courtesy from the bottom of my heart. The main reason I decided to edit this recipe book is that I wanted to collect as many fancy sweet recipes as I can and look at them whenever I like. Even though I am not cooking sweets daily anymore as I was in my childhood, seeing recipes is still quite enjoyable to me. Then, an idea came to me that I could make a recipe book that is less practical but more like 'reading' or 'picture-book,' assuming some other people also like the idea.
Again, thank you for visiting this website, and I hope you enjoy it.
